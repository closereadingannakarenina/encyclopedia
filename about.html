About Us

Contributors

This encyclopedia was produced by students in a course on Anna Karenina at Amherst College, Spring 2026.

Course Instructor

Maria Mayofis

Maria Mayofis is a literary scholar and cultural historian specializing in 19th- and 20th-century Russophone literature and culture, as well as contemporary children's literature in the post-Soviet space. Following an extensive career in Russia as an editor and university professor, she joined Amherst College, serving as a research fellow at the Center for Russian Culture from 2022 to 2024, and as a Visiting Assistant Professor of Russian from 2024 to 2026.
