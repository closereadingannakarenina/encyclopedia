About the Project

A Student Encyclopedia

Created for Anna Karenina: A Close Reading, taught by Professor Maria Mayofis.

This Encyclopedia is the collective project of a semester-long study of Leo Tolstoy's Anna Karenina, grounded in close-reading and external focus. Through discussion and analysis, the most significant elements of the novel were identified across four categories: key concepts, motifs, narrative devices, and historical contexts.
